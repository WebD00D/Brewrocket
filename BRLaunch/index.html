Your roadmap to homebrewing success

Our lessons are structured to take you from zero brewing knowledge to creating your very own recipes fast! With each lesson consisting of curated articles mixed in with exclusive video interviews of professionals explaining the topic, you’re sure to stay on the right track.

Learn and experiment

We'll send you a homebrew starter kit, as well as the ingredients needed to get brewing right away! You'll learn the fundamental skills needed to understand and brew a recipe, as well as gain the confidence to be able to start experimenting on your own.

Grow with a community

Don’t keep your new found brewing knowledge to yourself! Share with your friends and family, and teach others how as well! You'll be able to post questions and network with fellow members in Slack; sharing ideas and photos about your experiences brewing, and getting additional help.
