Welcome to Brewrocket!

You've been invited to participate in our beta!
We thank you for your interest in Brewrocket, and want to get you started right away, but before you dive in to your homebrewing adventure, let’s go over a few details of our test pilot program.

What is a test pilot? Am I qualified?
We are looking for early adopters who are interested in helping build the best online educational platform for homebrewing. Qualified test pilots are those that can give honest feedback, open to sharing ideas for features, and above all are genuinely interested in learning how to brew their own beer.

What does it cost, and what do I get?
Part of the Brewrocket mission is to create a low barrier to entry for those wanting to try their hand at homebrewing. For just $99, all test pilot’s will receive a homebrew starter kit with first batch ingredients included, full access to all features of the site, as well as free lifetime access to all future Brewrocket courses.

So what will I be test piloting?
All test pilots will be going through our introductory ‘Homebrewing 101’ course. As this course continues to evolve through user feedback, we will be taking notes and finalizing a curricula path that our intermediate and advanced courses can follow suit.

Remember, as a test pilot you will have free access to these courses, as well as some other special ‘early adopter perks’ once we move out of beta in a full release.
